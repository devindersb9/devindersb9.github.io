What We Are About

We started this business this year and have impressed many people who have tasted our Mediterranean Pizza that is now very well known in our community. We are very proud that our pizza has put smiles on a lot of peoples faces and will continue to do this as long as we can.

Our business started with 4 brilliant minds Balkirn, Sierra, Mikayla, Ravleen. We created this pizza business knowing the tough challenges we would face with larger companies having more customers, supplies, and locations. We started selling our famous Mediterranean pizza out of 1 small shop with little to no funds and we payed for our supplies paycheck by paycheck. Slowly as word got out our profits sky rocketed and we expanded our store, this exposure landed us a licensing deal with Pizza Hut and we were able to sell our recipe to the company for one milion dollars. We plan to use this money to expand our amount of locations, supplies, and employees for better service in the near future.
